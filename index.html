Our services

We've placed candidates looking for temporary and permanent roles in a wide range of departments, from Human
Resources, Marketing, Accounts and Finance, IT, Executive and Clerical support and Management. Our clients come
from a vast selection of industries including Legal, Education, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Utilities, Financial to name
just a few. You'll find us in Edinburgh, Manchester, Birmingham and London - and everywhere in between.

Marketing | HR | Account & Finance | IT | Executive Management

Whether you're searching for a challenging PA role or making the leap to marketing, HR or finance
management, our passionate, personable consultants will help you clinch your next career step.
